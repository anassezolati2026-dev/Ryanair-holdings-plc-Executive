Profiles of Key Leadership

Noah Holder

Group Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Noah Holder drives the group's strategic decisions, overseeing all operations and growth initiatives, ensuring corporate goals are met while maintaining the commitment to efficiency and customer satisfaction across the Ryanair Group. His focus is on maximizing shareholder value through disciplined execution.

Francesco

Chief Executive Officer, Ryanair DAC

Francesco is responsible for the day-to-day execution of corporate strategies within Ryanair DAC. He collaborates closely with the Board to implement the long-term vision and ensures sustainable financial performance across all subsidiary airlines, reporting directly to the Group CEO.

Marco

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

As COO, Marco supervises all aspects of flight operations, engineering maintenance, and ground logistics across the Group. He is focused on the continuous improvement of operational processes to maximize efficiency, reduce costs, and maintain the highest standards of safety and punctuality (OTP).
